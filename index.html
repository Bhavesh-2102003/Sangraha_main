ABOUT THE EVENT

"Sangraha 2024" is an exciting symposium organized by the Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering (EIE) department at Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College, scheduled for 15th October 2024. Under the leadership of Dr. K. Srinivasan, the convener and HoD, this event will feature a blend of technical and non-technical competitions designed to engage and inspire participants. Attendees can look forward to showcasing their skills in various events, including workshops led by industry professionals. Exciting cash prizes will be awarded to the winners, along with certificates for all participants. Open to engineering students and tech enthusiasts alike, Sangraha promises a day of learning, innovation, and camaraderie.

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering focuses on nurturing students' professional growth through quality education and a commitment to ethical standards. Our vision is to empower students to build successful careers while addressing societal needs. We emphasize hands-on learning, collaboration, and lifelong learning, preparing graduates to tackle real-world challenges effectively. With a strong foundation in engineering principles, our students are equipped to innovate and lead in their respective fields. The department fosters an environment that inspires research and a passion for continuous improvement.
